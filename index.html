Islamabad Security Dialogue 2022

Pakistan is hosting its first-ever security dialogue aimed to define the country’s new strategic direction under PM’s vision of peace,
regional connectivity, and development partnerships with the world. The two-day conference is being organized by the National Security
Division in collaboration with its advisory board. The platform comprises five leading think tanks including the Centre for Aerospace and Security Studies,
Islamabad Policy Research Institute, Institute of Strategic Studies, Institute of Regional Studies, and National Defense University’s Institute of Strategic Studies, Research, and Analysis.
The dedicated platform aims for collaborations on the subject of national security in which think tanks will share policy recommendations directly with the state leadership.
Chief of Army Staff General Qamar Javed Bajwa will be guest of honor in the ceremony while federal ministers, as well as members of the academia and media, will also participate.

Next meeting will be at:

    Karachi
    Multan
    Lahore
    Faisalabad
